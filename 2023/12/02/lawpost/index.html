Application of Large Language Models in Legal Settings

Fundamentally, LLMs can be extremely useful in the legal sector where gathering information and evidence from a large pool of information sources is crucial. LLMs, given their capabilities to extract and summarize important information from a large pool of text information can come in handy in helping extremely busy lawyers to reduce the amount of time they have to spend gathering information manually.

Moreover, the legal sector frequently deals with contracts and agreements. Specifically, identifying logical flaws and loopholes is a common requirement. These flaws often serve as pivotal evidence for lawyers. Manual identification of such issues can be tedious and time-consuming. Leveraging the quick and accurate text processing capabilities of LLMs streamlines this task can enhance work efficiency for legal professionals.
