Application of Large Language Models in Financial Settings

Currently, in the financial sector, customer support involves employing humans to answer frequently asked questions. Human staff are often tied up with these routine tasks, leaving them with less time to handle more complex issues. In customer support, LLMs replace humnas in repetitive tasks, answering customer queries and solving prolems that customers face. LLMs can do this by tapping on their immense potential in information processing, text generation and question answering. This allows human staff to focus on solving trickier problems, improving overall efficiency.

Presently, financial risk assessment involves analysis of a customer’s financial history and other factors to determine the risk associated with offering them financial products. LLMs can streamline this process by generating a more comprehasive analysis of these factors, improving the accuracy of risk assessments. Moreover, when it comes to providing personalized financial advice, we can train LLMs to understand a wider scope of information with regards to a customer’s financial preferences and tendencies. This enables them to generate tailored recommendations for investments, retirement planning etc., offering a more individualized approach compared to traditional methods.
